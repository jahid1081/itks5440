About

I am a highly skilled IT Officer with extensive experience in the banking sector, where I held the position of Executive Officer at United Commercial Bank PLC in Bangladesh. I resigned to pursue a Master's Degree in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Jyväskylä, a choice driven by my commitment to advancing my expertise in this vital field.

In my previous roles as Access Control Analyst and Business Analyst, I successfully managed critical applications, including the Core Banking System and Anti-Money Laundering Suite, while playing a pivotal role in developing UDispatch—an innovative in-house application for tracking organizational correspondence.

My passion for technology extends beyond banking. I taught Computer Science for Cambridge IGCSE O and A Levels at Scholastica, one of Bangladesh's most esteemed English-medium schools, and I served as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant at North South University in Bangladesh. With proficiency in C++, Python, Java, MATLAB, and Microsoft Office, I am dedicated to harnessing technology to enhance business processes and educational frameworks, particularly cybersecurity and IT governance.
